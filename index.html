Frequent asked questions

Q.What is the purpose of Event?

A.This event is to discuss TensorFlow updates. To demonstrate various uses of TensorFlow.

Q.Who is the audience of Event?

A.Anyone can attend. We welcome everyone to attend the event. Also, we would require your cooperation in adhering to our Community Guidelines.

Q.What are event Charges?

A. This event is completely free of charge.

Q.Will there be moderators

A.Yes! We have dedicated volunteers who will moderate all the sessions. If you witness any attendee breaching the code of conduct, please reach out to us at tensorflowindia@gmail.com

Q.Any prerequirement for attendeding event

A. There are no prerequirements for being part of the event. Every one who has interest on Machine Learning , Artificial Inteligence can participate in event.
